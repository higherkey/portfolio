SENSORY PLAY

Kids Games is a suite of interactions designed for the youngest users. By focusing on immediate haptic and visual responses to touch, we create a digital environment that feels as reactive as physical toys.

PHYSICS LOGIC

Using Matter.js for 2D physics allows us to create puzzles that feel natural. Balloons pop, balls bounce, and blocks stack with a level of realism that encourages exploration and discovery.
