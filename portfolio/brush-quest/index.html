THE QUEST

Brush Quest was developed to solve the "2-minute struggle" parents face every morning and night. By framing toothbrushing as a timed adventure with visual rewards, we turn a chore into a challenge.

HABIT LOOP

The technical core of the app is a zero-latency audio engine that syncs perfectly with character animations. This feedback loop is critical for maintaining child engagement throughout the full 120-second duration.
