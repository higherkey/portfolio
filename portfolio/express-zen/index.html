THE ZEN

Regular Expressions are notoriously difficult to read and maintain. Express Zen uses real-time parsing and AI-driven explanations to break down complex patterns into manageable, visual components.

CLEAR PATTERNS

I utilized a recursive decent parser to map regex tokens to SVG nodes. This allows for a hierarchical view of the pattern, making it easy to identify capture groups, quantifiers, and anchors at a glance.
