THE JOURNEY

Route 66 Passport was designed to bring the traditional 'stamp book' experience into the digital age. By using the browser's Geolocation API, users can 'check in' at designated landmarks and unlock unique, neon-styled SVG stamps.

RETRO FUTURE

The design system, 'Neon Americana', blends 1950s diner aesthetics with modern high-fidelity digital interfaces. We use CSS variables to create a flickering neon effect and deep, atmospheric backgrounds.
